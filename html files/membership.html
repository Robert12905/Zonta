Become A Member

Why Join Zonta?

  • Connect with professional women committed to service.
  • Make a local and global impact through advocacy and hands-on projects.
  • Develop leadership skills and lifelong friendships.
  • Support initiatives that advance women’s rights worldwide.

Membership Benefits

  • Monthly meetings & networking events
  • Volunteer opportunities in Naples-area service projects
  • Access to Zonta International initiatives and conferences
  • Leadership training and committee involvement
  • Exclusive member newsletters and updates

Member Expectations

  • Attend monthly club meetings
  • Participate in at least one service or fundraising project
  • Annual membership dues
  • Uphold Zonta’s mission and values

Types of Membership

  • Individual Member: Standard membership for local residents.
  • Young Professional Member: Reduced dues for ages 18–30.
  • Corporate Member: For businesses supporting Zonta’s mission.
  • Lifetime Member: Long-term supporters (if applicable).

How to Join

  1. Complete the membership application.
  2. Attend a meeting or meet with a membership coordinator.
  3. Submit dues.
  4. Receive welcome packet & member mentor.
