About Our Scholarships

View Details

The Zonta Club of Naples offers scholarships to outstanding women pursuing higher education. Our scholarships support academic excellence, leadership, and community service.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Female students residing in Naples or Collier County
  • High school seniors, college students, or adult learners
  • Strong academic performance
  • Demonstrated leadership and community involvement

How to Apply

  • Download the application form from our website.
  • Complete all sections of the form.
  • Submit your application along with transcripts and recommendation letters.
  • Submit before the deadline: April 30th.
